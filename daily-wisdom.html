Front Door Feng Shui

Your front door is where energy enters your home. Keep it clean, well-lit, and free of obstacles. A cracked or squeaky door repels good luck. A bright entrance invites prosperity energy.

Feng Shui
🔑

Where to Keep Your Keys

Don’t leave keys scattered —it scatters your focus. Keep them in a dedicated bowl near the entrance. Feng shui says this anchors your “decision energy” and prevents anxiety.

Feng Shui
🛏

Best Direction to Sleep

In feng shui, your head direction matters. Generally, head north benefits those born under water signs; east is good for wood; south for fire. But your personal Ba Zi chart gives the precise best direction.

Feng Shui
📱

Charging Phone Overnight

Don’t charge your phone next to your bed while you sleep —the electromagnetic energy disrupts sleep quality. Place it at least 1 meter from your pillow, on airplane mode if possible.

Feng Shui
🍀

Lucky Number for Today

Your lucky number changes daily based on the cosmic energy of the day. Check the lunar calendar: odd days favor action, even days favor reflection. Your personal lucky number from your birth chart amplifies this.

Daily Luck
⏰

Best Time to Start Something New

In Chinese metaphysics, timing matters. The best window to start a new project is during the first 3 hours after sunrise. Avoid starting major things after 3pm unless necessary —afternoon favors completion, not initiation.

Timing
🪞

Mirrors Facing the Bed

A mirror facing your bed creates restless energy —it reflects and doubles the qi, disturbing sleep. If this is unavoidable, cover the mirror with a cloth when sleeping.

Feng Shui
🎋

Plants and Their Direction

Plants bring wood energy. Place them in the east (health), southeast (wealth), or east corners of your home. Avoid too many plants in the south —fire + wood can create excessive energy that triggers arguments.

Feng Shui
💬

Avoid Major Decisions on These Days

In the Chinese lunar calendar, certain days are “void of content” (kong wang —energy is unstable and decisions made on these days tend to change. Check your lunar calendar before signing contracts or making big calls.
