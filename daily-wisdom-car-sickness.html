The 070 Image Number for Car Sickness

A simple Daoist Ba Gua image number therapy: when car sickness strikes, silently recite 070. Ancient wisdom, immediate practice.

Car sickness is not about the car. In the Daoist Ba Gua image number framework, car sickness is a specific energetic imbalance — and it has a specific number that resolves it. The practice is simple: when nausea strikes, close your eyes and silently recite 070. No tools, no preparation, no equipment. Just a number, a breath, and your body's own response.

0 · 7 · 0
The Car Sickness Image Number

The Story: A Real Case on New Year's Eve

The practical use of this number was demonstrated during a real journey. A group was traveling to Sentosa for New Year's Eve in Singapore — their first Chinese New Year away from home. The地铁 (subway) route was lined with turns and stops. One member of the group — we'll call her Xingchen — was severely affected by motion sickness on the subway, a rare and particularly acute form.

She could not even manage two consecutive stops without needing to get off and recover. Multiple taxi attempts were no better — she vomited so violently that the nausea喷射 (projected) from both nose and mouth, over a meter away.

By the time they finally reached the beach at Sentosa, Xingchen was pale, barely able to walk, and had been sick multiple times. At this point, the Daoist Ba Gua image number for car sickness was recalled: 070.

How to Use the 070 Number

Step 1: At the first sign of car sickness — nausea, dizziness, cold sweats — close your eyes. External visual stimulation worsens motion sickness by creating conflicting signals between your inner ear and your eyes.

Step 2: Begin silently reciting the number sequence: zero... seven... zero... in a calm, rhythmic manner. Do not speak it aloud — the practice requires inner recitation. The rhythm matters more than the speed.

Step 3: Continue for 3-5 minutes, or until symptoms begin to subside. Most people report noticeable relief within 5-10 minutes.

Step 4: Once symptoms ease, continue the practice for another 2-3 minutes before slowly stopping. Do not abruptly stop — ease out of the practice.

The Daoist Principle Behind It

In Ba Gua image number therapy, each number corresponds to a specific energetic quality and organ relationship. The number 070 addresses the specific energetic disruption caused by vehicle motion — the conflict between vestibular signals and visual field stability. By reciting the number, you redirect the mind's attention to an internal focal point, which helps re-regulate the nervous system's response to motion.

Why It Works: The Daoist Medicine Explanation

Motion sickness in Daoist medicine is understood as a disruption of the 胃气 (stomach qi) — the upward rebellion of stomach energy. The spleen and stomach are responsible for receiving and transforming food and drink. When the body is in motion, this transforming function is disrupted, causing nausea and vomiting as the stomach qi rebels upward.

The number 070 works through the principle of 象数感应 (image-number resonance) — the idea that specific numbers carry specific energetic qualities that can interact with the body's own energetic patterns. The number:

· 7 corresponds to the 艮卦 (Gen hexagram) — representing the stomach and digestive system in the Ba Gua system
· 0 functions as a stabilizing, grounding element — bridging and harmonizing the surrounding numbers
· The sequence 070 creates a balanced energetic formula: activation (7), grounding (0), return (0)

This is not magical thinking. From a modern neuroscience perspective, the internal focus required by silently reciting a number engages the prefrontal cortex, which has an inhibitory effect on the nausea response generated by the brainstem. It is essentially a form of directed attention that interrupts the nausea loop.

Other Useful Image Numbers in the Same Tradition

The Ba Gua image number therapy system includes many numbers for different conditions. While we present 070 specifically for car sickness, the broader system covers a wide range of common health issues. This is part of why the system is considered so practical — it requires no tools, no herbs, no equipment.

"八卦象数疗法，简单，方便，随时随地可以使用。不用针，不用药，一个数字即可。" — Ba Gua image number therapy: simple, convenient, usable anywhere. No needles, no medicine. Just a number.

A Practical Note

This remedy works best for uncomplicated motion sickness in otherwise healthy individuals. For severe, projectile vomiting that continues despite the practice, or for signs of dehydration, medical care should be sought. This is a self-care tool for mild to moderate cases — not a substitute for professional medical attention when symptoms are severe.
