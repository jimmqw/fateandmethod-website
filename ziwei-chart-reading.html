How to Read a Chart Step by Step

A practical guide to reading your Zi Wei Dou Shu chart. From identifying key palaces to analyzing star combinations.

Home / Zi Wei / Chart Reading

Reading a Zi Wei Dou Shu chart can seem overwhelming with all its stars and palaces. But by following a systematic approach, you can learn to read your chart effectively.

Step 1: Identify the Life Palace

The Life Palace (Ming Gong) is the foundation of your chart. It represents your core personality and overall life path.

  • Find your birth year stem and branch
  • Locate the palace where your Ming Gong is placed
  • Identify the main star in that palace

Step 2: Analyze the 14 Main Stars

Each main star brings specific energies. Understanding what each star represents helps you interpret its influence.

  • Zi Wei - Leadership, authority
  • Tian Ji - Wisdom, strategy
  • Tai Yang - Energy, fame
  • Wu Qu - Finance, career
  • And more...

Step 3: Check the Four Transformations

The Si Hua (Four Transformations) show where energy is activated in your chart:

  • Lu (Prosperity) - Material luck
  • Quan (Power) - Authority
  • Ke (Noble) - Mentors, fame
  • Ji (Obstacle) - Challenges

Step 4: Read Each Palace

Analyze the palaces relevant to your questions:

  • Life Palace (1) - Self, personality
  • Wealth Palace (2) - Finances
  • Relationship Palace (7) - Marriage
  • Fame Palace (9) - Career

Step 5: Look for Combinations

Stars interact with each other. Certain combinations create specific effects:

  • Zi Wei + Tian Ji - Leadership combination
  • Tai Yang + Wu Qu - Fame and wealth
  • Tan Lang + Ju Men - Creative expression

Key Takeaway

Start with understanding your Life Palace and the main star within it. Then expand to other relevant palaces based on your questions.
