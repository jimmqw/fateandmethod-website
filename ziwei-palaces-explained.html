The 12 Palaces Explained

Complete guide to the 12 Palaces - the foundation of Zi Wei Dou Shu chart reading.

In Zi Wei Dou Shu, the life chart is divided into 12 Palaces, each representing a different aspect of life. Understanding these palaces is essential for reading your destiny.

Detailed Palace Meanings

1. Life Palace

Represents self, personality, and overall life destiny. The most important palace.

2. Wealth Palace

Represents finances, income, and wealth accumulation.

3. House Palace

Represents property, real estate, and living environment.

4. Parents Palace

Represents parents, inheritance, and education.

5. Children Palace

Represents children, offspring, and creative expression.

6. Health Palace

Represents health, illness, and physical challenges.

7. Relationship Palace

Represents marriage and romantic relationships.

8. Siblings Palace

Represents siblings, friends, and colleagues.

9. Fame Palace

Represents career, reputation, and social status.

10. Fortune Palace

Represents fortune, luck, and enjoyment.

11. Travel Palace

Represents travel, migration, and external opportunities.

12. Hidden Palace

Represents hidden matters and spiritual concerns.

How to Read Palaces

When analyzing any palace, consider:

  • Ruling star: Which main star occupies the palace
  • Visiting stars: Other stars that enter the palace
  • Transformations: Whether Si Hua appears in the palace
