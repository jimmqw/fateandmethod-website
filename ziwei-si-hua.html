Zi Wei Dou Shu

The Four Transformations

Understanding Lu (Prosperity), Quan (Power), Ke (Noble), and Ji (Obstacle) - the core of Zi Wei Dou Shu reading.

The Four Transformations (Si Hua) are the core concept in Zi Wei Dou Shu astrology. They determine how stars change their nature and influence different life areas.

What Are the Four Transformations?

The Four Transformations include:

  • Lu (化禄) - Prosperity, wealth, material enjoyment
  • Quan (化权) - Power, authority, competition
  • Ke (化科) - Noble, fame, education
  • Ji (化��) - Obstacle, restriction, attachment

1. Lu (化禄) - Prosperity

Nature: Prosperity, wealth, income

  • Represents salary, investments, business income
  • Represents material comfort and lifestyle
  • Represents romantic luck

2. Quan (化权) - Power

Nature: Authority, power, competition

  • Represents authority and status
  • Represents competition and rivalry
  • Represents leadership abilities

3. Ke (化科) - Noble

Nature: Fame, education, mentors

  • Represents fame and recognition
  • Represents mentors and helpful people
  • Represents exams and education

4. Ji (化忌) - Obstacle

Nature: Obstacles, restrictions

  • Represents challenges and setbacks
  • Represents attachment and obsession
  • Represents restrictions
