How to Read a Zi Wei Dou Shu Chart

Step-by-step practical guide: put the stars, palaces, and combinations together to read any chart.

You have learned the 14 main stars. You have mastered the 12 palaces. You understand star combinations. Now comes the real skill: reading them together, in context, as a unified whole. This is where Zi Wei Dou Shu transforms from a list of traits into a living portrait of a human being. This guide takes you through the complete reading process, step by step.

Before You Begin: The Three-Layer Structure

Layer 3: Hour Stem 鈥?Your birth hour determines which specific stars go into which specific palaces. This is what makes your chart uniquely yours.

Begin every reading by identifying which layer is creating the feature you are examining.
